3 Bucket Challenge

A framework for your role in an AI world

To complete the 3 Bucket Challenge, divide your workload as follows:

Bucket 1: Routine tasks that are roughly 80% automatable.
Bucket 2: High-level work involving strategy, taste, and decision-making.
Bucket 3: Personal work that only you can do, fueled by your unique background and character.

01 —

Tasks AI Can Do 80% as Well

Examples
  • Basic research and info gathering
  • Creating outlines from notes
  • Creating action items from meetings
  • Drafting routine emails
02 —

Taste, Judgement & Strategic Vision

Examples
  • Curating info for specific audiences
  • Deciding which projects to prioritize and why
  • Knowing when 'good enough' is sufficient
  • Identifying which emerging trends matter
03 —

Personality or Lived Experience

Examples
  • Building trust through authenticity
  • The questions you ask that others don't think to ask
  • Sharing personal anecdotes
  • Your particular approach to conflict resolution
