3 Bucket Challenge

a future proof exercise

01
Bucket One 01 —

Tasks AI Can Do
80% as Well

Routine tasks that are roughly 80% automatable — let AI carry the weight here.

Examples
  • Basic research and info gathering
  • Reformatting content across formats
  • Editing content drafts
  • Summarizing long documents or reports into key takeaways
  • Building comparison tables or pros/cons lists from research
  • Creating agendas from scattered prep notes
  • Drafting routine emails
02
Bucket Two 02 —

Taste, Judgement &
Strategic Vision

High-level work involving strategy, taste, and decision-making — where human discernment matters most.

Examples
  • Curating info for specific audiences
  • Deciding which projects to prioritize and why
  • Knowing when 'good enough' is sufficient
  • Reading the room in a meeting and adjusting your approach on the fly
  • Recognizing when a process is broken
  • Knowing when to push back on a request vs. just get it done
  • Understanding the unwritten politics around a decision
03
Bucket Three 03 —

Personality or
Lived Experience

Personality or lived experience fueled by your unique background and character — irreplaceable and distinctly human.

Examples
  • Building trust through authenticity
  • The questions you ask that others don't think to ask
  • Your vibe
  • Mentoring someone through a career decision
  • Your instinct for when humor will land vs. when it won't
  • The connections you draw between unrelated ideas
  • Knowing which hill to die on and which to let go

Step 2

Optimize for Buckets 2 & 3,
learn to orchestrate Bucket 1

"In the Age of AI, intelligence is cheap. Judgement, Taste and Lived Experience is what matters."
